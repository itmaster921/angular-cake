FAQs

How do I place an order?

Orders can be placed from this website, Whatsapp at (246) 269-5987 or via Facebook direct message.

How early must an order be placed?

Orders must be placed at least 1 week (7 days) in advance. A late fee of $20-$25 is added when orders are made in 3 days or less from the due date.

Can my order be delivered?

There is no delivery service offered at this time.

Does a deposit have to be made?

All orders over $85 require a 50% deposit of the total price.

How to cancel an order?

Any cancellations to an order must be done at least 2 days before the due date.
